The carousel redesign transforms the dashboard from displaying generic feature announcements to providing meaningful, actionable patient status data. Guardians now have immediate access to:

2. **Weekly Trends**: Short-term patterns

4. **Behavioral Insights**: Triggers and effective strategies

This redesign significantly improves the clinical value and user experience of the guardian dashboard.
